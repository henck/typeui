## align 

`Card.Content` and `Card.Header` elements can have horizontal alignment.

export const AlignTemplate = (args) =>
<Card {...args}>
  <Card.Header align="right">
    Card header
  </Card.Header>
  <Card.Content align="right">
    Card content
  </Card.Content>
</Card>

## secondary 

A `Card.Content` element can be made `secondary` and will have a darker background.

<Card {...args}>

    Card content one.
  </Card.Content>
  <Card.Content secondary>
    Card content two.
  </Card.Content>
</Card>
