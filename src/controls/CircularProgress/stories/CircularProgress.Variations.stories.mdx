## animated

A `CircularProgress` can be `animated`. When it first appears, it will 
animate its progress line from 0 to `value`.

  <Story
    name="animated"
    args={{animated: true, value: 50}}>
    {Template.bind({})}
  </Story>
</Canvas>

## background 

With `background` set, `CircularProgress` will add a gray background to 
its progress line.

<Canvas>
  <Story
    name="background"
    args={{background: true, value: 25}}>

  </Story>
